Why Visit Toronto?

A A A

Toronto's waterfront - one of North America's largest recreational waterfronts - provides a scenic backdrop for many popular and entertaining attractions including the Harbourfront, Rogers centre, the Cn Tower and the Toronto Islands. It's also the setting of many big events including the Scotiabank the Torronto Internations Film Festival, the Canadian National Exhibition, Caribbean Carnival and Winterlicious.
